Hello! I'm Mark Jones. I'm a postdoc at Delft University of Technology, working in Discrete Mathematics & Optimization group within DIAM. I also run the group's semi-regular seminar series.. Most of my research concerns algorithmic approaches to problems in phylogenetics, with a particular focus on phylogenetic networks and parameterized complexity.
